Bérengère's collection

A small gathering of some of my all time favourite movies, 100% would recommend!

Horror

Psycho

The Shining

Stanley Kubrick
Jack and his family move into an isolated hotel with a violent past. Living in isolation, Jack begins to lose his sanity, which affects his family members.
Sci-Fi Comedy

Back To The Future

Robert Zemeckis
Marty travels back in time using an eccentric scientist's time machine. However, he must make his high-school-aged parents fall in love in order to return to the present.
Sci-Fi Space Opera

Star Wars EpIV : A New Hope

George Lucas
After Princess Leia, the leader of the Rebel Alliance, is held hostage by Darth Vader, Luke and Han Solo must free her and destroy the powerful weapon created by the Galactic Empire.
Fantasy Comedy

Charlie & The Chocolate Factory

Tim Burton
Charlie, a young boy from an impoverished family, and four other kids win a tour of an amazing chocolate factory run by an imaginative chocolatier, Willy Wonka, and his staff of Oompa-Loompas
Documentary Murder

American Murder: The Family Next Door

NETFLIX
American Murder: The Family Next Door is an American true crime documentary film directed by Jenny Popplewell. The film tells the story of the 2018 Watts family murders, which took place in Frederick, Colorado.
History Drama

The Other Boleyn Girl

Justin Chadwick
Centred on the the court of Henry VIII, this historical drama presents a calculating Anne, who usurps her older sister Mary as the king's mistress and ultimately desires to be his new queen.
Anime Fantasy

Spirited Away

Hayao Miyazaki
Hayao Miyazaki, 10-year-old Chihiro and her parents stumble upon a seemingly abandoned amusement park. After her mother and father are turned into giant pigs, Chihiro meets the mysterious Haku, who explains that the park is a resort for supernatural beings who need a break from their time spent in the earthly realm, and that she must work there to free herself and her parents.
Documentary Society

The Social Dilemma

NETFLIX
Tech experts sound the alarm on the dangerous human impact of social networking.
Satire Comedy

The Meaning Of Life

Monty Python
The stages of life are told through multiple sketches and songs by the British comedy troupe. The seven parts of life cover birth, growing up, war, middle age, organ transplants, old age and death. Not all stages are singular: "Part I: The Miracle of Birth" is from the perspective of an ignored woman in labor, and of a Roman Catholic couple with too many children, and "Part VII: Death" encompasses a funeral and heaven. Added are three unrelated skits placed in the beginning, middle and end.
Sci-Fi Horror

LIFE

Daniel Espinosa
As astronauts discover the first evidence of extra-terrestrial life on Mars, they begin realising that the life form is extremely intelligent and hostile.
